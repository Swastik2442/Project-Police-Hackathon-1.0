Police Feedback System

The Project by team Binary Brigade, for the showcase at Rajasthan Police Hackathon 1.0, is all about making things better between the police and the community.
"We've created a system that's easy for everyone to use, lets people share feedback in real-time, keeps things secure, and helps police understand what the community needs. It's all about working together!"

Submission Portal Analysis System

Features

Submission Portal

Empower community engagement by providing a user-friendly platform for citizens to submit valuable feedback, enhancing transparency and collaboration between the police and the public.

SMS System

Enhance accessibility with a seamless SMS feedback mechanism, allowing citizens to easily share their experiences and concerns with law enforcement, fostering a direct and immediate communication channel.

Chat Bot

Introduce a cutting-edge Chat Bot for instant interaction, answering queries, and guiding users through the feedback process. Simplify communication and bridge the gap between the community and law enforcement.

Bilingual Support

Fostering inclusivity by incorporating Bilingual Support, allowing citizens to submit feedback comfortably in their preferred language, enhancing communication and understanding between the community and law enforcement.

Mobile-first Approach

Revolutionizing accessibility and prioritizing convenient community engagement through smartphones. Ensuring citizens can easily connect, submit feedback, and participate in shaping effective policing on-the-go.

Analysis System

Harness the power of data analytics with a Feedback Analysis Portal, providing law enforcement agencies with valuable insights derived from citizen feedback. Facilitate informed decision-making and continuous improvement in community policing.
